# Privacy Policy for WiseShield

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

WiseShield ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Service Data
To provide real-time phishing protection, the extension sends the URLs of the websites you visit to our backend servers for analysis. 

### 1.2 Specifically, we collect:
- **URL features**: Length, character patterns, and domain structure.
- **Page Metadata**: Presence of certain form fields (e.g., password inputs).

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:
- **Phishing Detection**: To determine if a website is a threat.
- **Security Research**: To improve our detection algorithms and protect users from new threats.
- **System Performance**: To ensure the service is running correctly.

## 3. Data Retention and Sharing

- **No Personal Identification**: We do not associate URLs with your personal identity, email address, or IP address in our logs.
- **No Selling of Data**: We never sell your browsing data or personal information to third parties.
- **Data Sharing**: We do not share your data with any third parties except as required by law.

## 4. Your Choices
You can disable the extension or "Standalone Mode" at any time through the extension settings, which will stop all data transmission to our servers.

## 5. Security
We implement industry-standard security measures (including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit) to protect your information.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at security@phishguard.example.com.
